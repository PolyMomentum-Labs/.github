# Code of Conduct

PolyMomentum is an open and welcoming community. We expect all participants to treat each other with respect.

## Our standards

We aim for:

- Respectful, inclusive communication
- Constructive feedback and good-faith collaboration
- Clear, honest disclosure of risks when discussing trading and automation

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, doxxing, and encouraging unsafe or illegal activity.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

To report an incident, contact the maintainers privately.
